LicensingÔÉÅ

One of the more pressing requirements for kit registration is clearly identifying the license that is applied to your code. This is typically included in a LICENSE file at the top level of your repository. Without a license, the only assumption a user can make about your code is that they are not in a position to use your code.

Your license needs to be compatible with the GPLv2+ license currently used by MDAnalysis, in addition to the licenses of any other packages your MDAKit depends on. Take time to consider how you would like to license your project.
